## Welcome to Day 4

WELCOME TO DAY 4

We're nearly halfway through our module and we on the teaching team continue to be inspired by your enthusiasm for the course and your thoughtful engagement with your peers.  Thank you!  

Here in day 4 you'll have the opportunity to synthesize your learning thus far through three activities and a mid-module assessment found in a separate tab under the day 4 link on the left.  We hope all of these exercises will help you deepen your understanding of the cultural studies method we've been exploring.  On Day 5 we'll begin to apply this method specifically to issues and illustrations related to scriptures.

The decision to increase the number of discussion threads seems to have enhanced opportunities for more responsive interactions among you all and we hope you agree.   Please remember that we've also added another live discussion section on Friday, March 11 at 10:00am EST in addition to the one previously scheduled at 3:00pm EST. I hope all of you who are interested in these live discussions opportunities will be able to attend one of these sessions. Our prompt for this week is the same as option two for the mid-module assessment:  Use the following intro line: "Did you know...?" and complete the question with an idea or fact that you've learned so far that you find especially compelling and important for others to know. Be prepared to share a brief comment on why you find it compelling and why others should care. We look forward to seeing you then!

Thanks again for your continued engagement with the course and with each other.  It is a pleasure and privilege to be on this journey together.  

Professor Moore
